# Privacy Policy - GK Summary Extension

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## Overview

GK Summary ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The Extension operates entirely within your browser.

### What the Extension Does:
- Reads the text content of web pages you visit (only when you click "Summarize" or "Extract Key Points")
- Stores your preferences (language, theme, exam type) locally in your browser
- Stores saved summaries and key points locally in your browser

### What the Extension Does NOT Do:
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not use analytics or telemetry
- Does not share data with third parties
- Does not use cookies

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|-----------|----------------|
| `activeTab` | To read the current page content when you click "Summarize" |
| `storage` | To save your preferences and bookmarked summaries locally |
| `scripting` | To extract text content from web pages for summarization |
| `contextMenus` | To provide right-click menu options for quick summarization |

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API:
- User preferences (language, theme, exam type)
- Saved summaries and key points
- No data is stored on external servers

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time:
1. Open the Extension popup
2. Go to "Saved" tab
3. Click "Clear All"

Or clear all extension data:
1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Find "GK Summary"
3. Click "Details" → "Clear data"

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools.
